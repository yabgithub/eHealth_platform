== Description ==
FSars Medical WordPress theme is beautiful & clean and its free multipurpose medical WordPress theme, Which is used most of Sars, Covid-19, general clinics, dental, hospital, gynecology, veterinary clinics, nursing home and overall medial websites as well as personal portfolio sites for doctors, surgeons, gynecologist, general therapist, and all medical sector people. Also hospitals, clinics, nursing homes, veterinary clinics, medical stores, ambulance services, pharmaceuticals, physiotherapy centers and spa and massage centers. This Free Medical WordPress theme is fully responsive, cross-browser compatible.  FSars Medical is supported to the latest WordPress version and easily customize all sections. Please check demo link :https://www.featherthemes.com/demo/fsars-medical-pro/, Product Pro url: https://www.featherthemes.com/themes/wordpress-fsars-medical/ , Documentaion urlhttps://www.featherthemes.com/documentation/fsars-medical-pro/, Support url: https://www.featherthemes.com/forums/forum/fsars-medical/ 

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= How to customize theme FSars Medical? =

Just click on Appearance -> Customize
Now you can customize all section in theme

= How to Upgrade theme to pro ?=
Just click on Appearance -> Customize 
Now find upgrade to pro button, just click on it to upgrade pro version

= How to work on Header Phone and Address =
Just click on Appearance -> Customize -> Header Phone and Address

= How to work on Header social links =
Just click on Appearance -> Customize -> FSars Medical Social Link

= How to work on Banner Text? =
Just click on Appearance -> Customize -> FSars Medical Home Banner Text

= How to work on Footer copyright ? =
Just click on Appearance -> Customize -> FSars Medical Footer
